About

Welcome to my website!

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Accounting at the Kellogg School of Management, Northwestern University, and will be on the 2025–2026 academic job market. I expect to graduate in May 2026.

In my job market paper, I show that firms invisible to alternative data are not immune to its effects, as they adjust their disclosure behavior in response to peer data availability.

My broader research interests lie in big data information dynamics and the influence of institutional investor behavior on corporate disclosure, governance, and innovation.
